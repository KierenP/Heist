Heist

A top down stealth shooter written in C++ with SFML.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Brief

Heist is a classic top down shooter. Two teams spawn in a closed map, and the team who gets the most kills in the specified time wins. Comes with basic Ai.
